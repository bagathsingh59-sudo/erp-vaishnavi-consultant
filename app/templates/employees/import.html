Import Guidelines
Required Fields
  • Establishment — must match name or PF code
  • Name, Father/Husband, Gender, DOB, DOJ
  • At least UAN or ESIC IP
Salary Types
  • Daily — fill Daily Rate + Weekly Off
  • Monthly — fill Gross Salary (single amount)
  • MonthlyHeads — fill Basic, DA, HRA, etc. columns (Gross auto-calculated)
  • CTC — fill CTC Amount only (system auto-splits into Basic, HRA, Other Allowance after deducting employer EPF/ESIC)
WO Policy & Notes
  • WO Applicable: Yes / No (blank = use establishment default)
  • WO Type: Paid / Unpaid
  • WO Day: Sunday, Monday, etc. or Rotational
  • Duplicates skipped (matched by UAN)
  • Names auto-converted to UPPERCASE
  • Dates: DD-MM-YYYY or DD/MM/YYYY
