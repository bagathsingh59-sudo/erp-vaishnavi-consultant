What is a Paid Leave Run? Computes annual Earned Leave with Wages per the Karnataka Factories Act (Sec. 79): sums each employee's attendance Jan → Dec, derives leave days (Total Attendance ÷ 20), and multiplies by the December daily rate to get the encashment amount. Eligible employees are those with ≥ 240 days worked.
