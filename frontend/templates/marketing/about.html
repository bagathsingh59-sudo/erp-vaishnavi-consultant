Our story

Twelve years of staying out of your way.

We started in 2013 with a single conviction: payroll and statutory compliance is too important to outsource to an anonymous national agency, and too tedious to leave on a small business owner's desk. The middle ground — a focused regional consultancy run by people who pick up the phone — is where the real value lives.

Today we manage payroll, EPF, ESIC, professional tax and statutory reporting for over three hundred establishments across Karnataka. From workshops in Wadi to security agencies in Bengaluru and civil contractors in Kalaburagi, the work looks different on the surface and identical underneath: file accurately, file on time, keep the record clean.

Our client portal — accessible from anywhere — is the latest chapter of that practice. It puts every document we generate for you in your own hands, indexed and downloadable, available the moment you need to send something to an auditor or a labour officer at midnight on a Sunday.

From the founder

"We answer the phone."

When I started this practice, I noticed something strange about the market: the bigger the compliance agency, the harder it was to talk to a human who actually knew your business. I built Vaishnavi Consultant to be the opposite of that — a firm deliberately scaled so every establishment owner knows the name of the consultant handling their file.

We've kept that promise for twelve years. The portal you can sign into now is just an extension of it — your records, in your hands, with a real consultant a phone call away when the records can't answer your question.

Founder, Vaishnavi Consultant
