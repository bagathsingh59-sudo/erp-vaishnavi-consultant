Supported Formats
  • Template format — Our standard import template
  • Your existing Excel — System auto-detects columns like companyName, epfCodeNumber, etc.

Important Notes
  • Company Name is the only required field
  • Duplicates are automatically skipped (matched by company name)
  • Dates can be in DD-MM-YYYY or DD/MM/YYYY format
  • PAN & GST are auto-converted to uppercase
  • Compliance Payment Mode — enter "Through Us" (default) or "Client Direct" (fee-only clients)
  • Opening Balance — optional ₹ amount with Type Dr (client owes) or Cr (excess)
