What is a Bonus Run? A Bonus Run calculates annual bonus for all employees of one establishment for one Financial Year (Apr → Mar). It reads your finalized monthly payrolls and computes bonus as Attendance × Daily Rate × Bonus % for each month, summed across all 12 months. No wage ceiling, no eligibility cap — only the Sec. 8 minimum days rule decides who is Eligible vs Not Eligible.
